if one of you is australian and the other isn't, sooner or later you meet the partner visa. and the first thing you learn is that the sticker price is not the price.

this is the guide we wish someone had handed us at the start. usual disclaimer, this is our research and lived experience, not immigration advice, fees change, always check the department of home affairs or a registered migration agent for your case.

> 📷 the folder. THE folder. every LDR couple with a visa application knows the folder

## the two pathways, in one table

| | subclass 820/801 | subclass 309/100 |
|---|---|---|
| where you apply | **onshore** (already in australia) | **offshore** (outside australia) |
| can you stay together while waiting | yes, bridging visa | not until it's granted |
| typical all-in cost | ~$9k-10k+ | ~$9k-10k+ |
| the wait | months to years | months to years |
| the vibe | together but in limbo | apart but with a date |

the application fee is the headline number, but it's not the total. here's what actually stacks up.

## what the money actually goes on

- **the application fee.** the big one, thousands of dollars, paid upfront, non-refundable. yes even if you get rejected. no we don't want to talk about it.
- **health checks.** panel doctor appointments, per person costs, and they expire if the wait runs long (see: the wait).
- **police certificates.** one per country you've lived in. lived in three countries? congratulations, three certificates, three fees, three different bureaucracies.
- **translations and certifications.** if any documents aren't in english.
- **the evidence.** printing, statutory declarations from friends, and the general admin of proving to a government that your love is real. surreal experience. keep every boarding pass.

> 💸 budget the application fee, then add 15-20% for the bits around it. that's the honest number. our calculator bakes an indicative all-in figure into any australia move so the surprise happens on a screen instead of your bank statement.

## the cost nobody puts in the brochure: the wait

processing times are measured in seasons. during that time you're either on a bridging visa figuring out work rights, or still apart paying for flights and two rents.

> ❝ the visa fee you pay once. the waiting you pay monthly.

that's why our [savings guide](/blog/how-much-savings-before-moving-in-together) treats the in-between buffer as its own bucket. for australia moves it's often bigger than the visa itself.

## what we'd tell past us

- **start the police certificates early.** they're slow and they're the thing that stalls applications.
- **screenshot everything as you go.** building two years of relationship evidence retroactively is misery. a shared album and the discord server export are gold.
- **date the finances.** joint accounts, shared bills, anything with both names and a date on it starts the clock on "genuine and continuing."
- **NZ citizens, breathe.** the trans-tasman arrangement means kiwis generally live and work in australia without any of this.

> pip's take: two humans paid thousands of dollars to prove they like each other. pip proved it by flying 8,000km with a heart in his beak. birds are more efficient.
